Humidity v. Latitude


For this exercise we were asked to evaluate the humidity in relation to the distance of the city to the equator. As the illustration shows, the max humidity anywhere is 100% (though it may feel like more).

The heaviest concentration of humidity was an even split on either side of the equator but at equidistance (20/-20, 40/-40).
